about us

Dream Construction Services LTD is a London based construction company with a team of highly skilled craftsmen and professionals ready to deliver any sized construction project from start to finish.

Whether it’s a new build, extension, loft conversion, or renovation, we can help, with a portfolio of building work across London.

We excel in working as part of a collaborative team to develop, design, and construct each project and we ensure our clients participate in the process every step of the way – consulting on the design, and achieving their goals for cost, schedule and quality.

Why Choose Us

WE ARE PASSIONATE

We love what we do and take great pride in all of our work. As a team we’re dedicated to ensuring your project is delivered on time and to the highest standard possible.

HONEST AND DEPENDABLE

For us, honesty is the only policy and we strive to complete all projects with integrity and professionalism. With many completed projects under our belt, we consider ourselves one of the most trusted build companies in UK.

WE ARE ALWAYS IMPROVING

We commit ourselves to complete all projects within the timeline set with our clients. We use the best of technology and tools to ensure that all jobs are done efficiently, but also giving attention to details to ensure everything is done correctly.

ATTENTION TO DETAIL

We aim to seamlessly blend new extensions to the original property by carefully selecting the correct materials so your extension looks like an original part of your existing home.
